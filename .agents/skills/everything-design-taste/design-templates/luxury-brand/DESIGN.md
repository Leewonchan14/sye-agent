# Luxury Brand Design System

## Color Tokens
```css
:root {
  --color-bg-primary: #0A0A0A;
  --color-bg-secondary: #141414;
  --color-bg-tertiary: #1F1F1F;
  --color-text-primary: #F5F0EB;
  --color-text-secondary: #A39E99;
  --color-accent: #C9A96E;
  --color-accent-hover: #B8953F;
  --color-border: #2A2A2A;
  --color-success: #4ADE80;
  --color-error: #F87171;
}
```

## Typography
- Display: "Cormorant Garamond", serif (300, 500)
- Body: "Libre Franklin", sans-serif (300, 400)
- Letter-spacing: 0.05em on all-caps labels

## Spacing
8 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64 / 96 / 128 (generous throughout)

## Radius
0px everywhere. Sharp corners. No softness.

## Shadows
None. Use subtle borders or background differentiation instead.

## Motion
- Micro: 200ms ease-out
- Standard: 400ms cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
- Emphasis: 600ms cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
- Everything moves slowly and deliberately.

## Personality
Restraint is the entire point. Say less. Show less. Let space do the talking. One element per viewport. Thin weights. Gold used sparingly, never as a background fill. The feeling of walking into a private members club, not a department store.
